## 🚀 About the Ecosystem

**Belilovsky Platform** is a vertically integrated technology ecosystem building Kazakhstan's next-generation digital infrastructure. We operate across **media**, **government analytics**, **AI/ML infrastructure**, and **communication strategy** — serving millions of readers, analysts, and government stakeholders.

Our stack spans the full pipeline: from **data ingestion** (QazPipe) → **storage & enrichment** (QazLake, AI summarization) → **NLP inference** (QazCompute) → **GIS services** (QazGeo) → **identity & access** (id.qdev.run) → **frontend delivery** (Total.kz, Ortcom.kz, Commentarius).
